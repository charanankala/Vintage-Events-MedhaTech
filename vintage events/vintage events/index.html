Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your events vintage-themed?

+

We specialize in authentic vintage styling using period-appropriate decorations, furniture, music, and cuisine from specific eras. Our team meticulously researches historical details to create genuine vintage atmospheres.

How far in advance should I book my vintage event?

+

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller events and 3-4 months for larger celebrations like weddings to ensure availability of vintage items and proper planning time.

Do you provide vintage costumes or attire guidance?

+

Yes! We offer styling consultations and can recommend period-appropriate attire. We also have partnerships with vintage clothing rental services to help you and your guests dress the part.

Can you accommodate different vintage eras?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in various vintage eras including Victorian, 1920s Art Deco, 1950s Retro, and 1960s themes. Each era has its unique style and we tailor everything accordingly.

What's included in your vintage catering service?

+

Our vintage catering includes period-appropriate menus, vintage serving dishes, classic cocktails, and presentation styles that match your chosen era. We use traditional recipes and serving methods for authenticity.
